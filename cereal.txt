A easy and delicious breakfast

What you need:
- 1 cup of 2% milk
- 1.5 cups of Cheerios

1. Add the 1.5 cups of Cheerios to a liquid safe bowl
2. Slowly pour the cup of milk over the cereal
Fun Hint: Only fill in the holes of the cereal to make a special and impressive treat
3. Serve and eat immediately!
4. Yum! Go for seconds!
